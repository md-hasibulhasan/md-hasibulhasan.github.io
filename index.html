Biography

Md Hasibul Hasan is a PhD candidate in Environmental Engineering at UCF air quality research Lab. His research interests include low cost sensor technology, air quality modeling, machine learning, motor vehicle emissions estimation, exposure estimation and GHG emission uncertainity estimation in aerial remote sensing. He is currently working on multiple projects funded by National Science Foundation (NSF), Florida Department of Transportation (FDOT), Metroplan Orlando (MPO), California Air Resource Board (CARB) and SailBri Cooper (SCI). He was a recipient of prestigious ORCGS Doctoral Fellowship from UCF.He received SAS data mining graduate certificate from University of Central Florida in 2023. He is a licenced Engineer in training (EIT) from Maryland Department of Labor.
